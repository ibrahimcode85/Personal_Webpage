Deadfish 10km Endurance Swim
15 December 2024
I had the opportunity to join my friends from my swimming club in this challenge to raise donations for Sunflower Mission, which supports children's education and those in need in Vietnam. This was my first time swimming a 10K distance, and I’m glad I could complete it despite having limited time to train. The event was held at Tasik Widuri, and I had a great time swimming with my clubmates.
