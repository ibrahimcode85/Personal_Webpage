Malaysia Freediving Championship 2024
9 November 2024
This was my very first freediving pool competition, held in Kuala Lumpur. I successfully performed clean dives in all three disciplines: Static (3 minutes 34 seconds), Dynamic Bi-Fins (99 meters), and Dynamic No-Fins (75 meters). Thrilled to have secured the second runner-up position in the men's overall category!
