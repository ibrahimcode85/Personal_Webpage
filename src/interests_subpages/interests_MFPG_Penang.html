MFPG Penang — Breaking Previous National Record (125m)
20 Dec 2025
I competed in the Malaysia Freediving Pool Games (MFPG) held in Penang, going in with a mix of excitement and nerves. Training leading up to the event was encouraging — consistently beyond the previous national record of 119m, with several dives at 125m and up to 139m. While I didn’t secure the official national record as it was later surpassed by another athlete, I’m grateful to have broken the previous mark at 125m under competition conditions. A meaningful milestone, and an important step in my journey.
Managed 125m in Dynamic No Fins dive.
White card received, marking the break of the previous national record.
Announcement on AIDA Malaysia’s Instagram for exceeding the previous national record.
