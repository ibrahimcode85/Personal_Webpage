Penang Freediving Pool Games
8 March 2025
Competed in a freediving competition held in Penang—a memorable experience, as it was my first time diving in a 50m pool and also my first time experiencing a blackout during a dive (I was fasting at the time). Although I was disqualified on Day 1 for my 109m attempt due to the blackout, I’m glad I managed to update my official no-fins record to 96m the next day. It was also great to reconnect with friends I met during my last trip to Penang for the DNF workshop.
